About This Website

Welcome to Nobel Numbat! This website is designed to provide users with insights into genetic data associated with Type 2 Diabetes, specifically within the South Asian population.

This project focuses on combining genetic association studies with population genomic data to help users understand the genetic basis of Type 2 Diabetes susceptibility.

Here you can retrieve information on:

  • Single Nucleotide Polymorphisms (SNPs) associated with T2D.
  • Mapped genes, genomic coordinates, and population statistics.
  • Visualizations for genomic regions of interest and machine learning techniques.
  • Downloadable reports and data for further analysis.

This project was developed as part of the MSc Bioinformatics Software Development Group Project.
